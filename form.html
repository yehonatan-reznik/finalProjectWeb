What Makes A Good Request
  • One issue at a time Split UI polish, detection quality, and controller behavior into separate requests when possible.
  • Use live terms Copy the exact text from the console or status panel so the issue maps back to a real screen state.
  • Show the failure A single screenshot or short log excerpt is usually better than a long description.

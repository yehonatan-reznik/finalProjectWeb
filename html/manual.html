Manual

Fast guidance for stream setup, manual control, and safe shutdown.

Dashboard Logs

1. Connect the feed

Paste the camera URL and confirm the live image appears.

2. Verify the controller

Connect the controller address and confirm the rig responds.

3. Follow target guidance

Use `x/y`, center offset, and move hints to keep the target centered.

Enter the camera URL as `http://camera-ip/stream`. When the image loads, detection starts automatically.

Connect the controller address and confirm arrows, `Laser On`, `Laser Off`, and `Stop Operation` respond correctly.

Leave `AeroYOLO ONNX` selected unless you need the generic COCO fallback. Thresholds only affect detection confidence.

When a target is tracked, read the label, confidence, `x/y`, and move hints together.

Fire only after visual confirmation. Use `Laser Off` immediately after action and center the rig before leaving.
